Paint Application

Probability Project

For this project, I created a sunburst diagram to visualize global energy consumption in 2023, comparing renewable and non-renewable energy sources. The diagram effectively breaks down total energy usage into two primary categories: Non-Renewable and Renewable, with further subdivisions representing specific energy sources such as oil, coal, natural gas, hydropower, and wind. Using an intuitive color scheme, the visualization makes it easy to see the dominance of non-renewable sources while highlighting the contribution of renewable energy.
